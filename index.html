About me

I studied physiotherapy at Robert Gordon University in Aberdeen, then worked in hospitals and sports injury clinics in Scotland before qualifying as a Chartered Physiotherapist in Animal Therapy in 2006. I've worked for established animal physiotherapy practices in Yorkshire and Hampshire alongside British equestrian team physiotherapists.

I started riding at the age of 3, and have ridden horses most of my life. I was fortunate to have the chance to represent Scotland in show jumping at national level, and also competed at Hickstead and the Horse of the Year Show.
